# Origin

This repo was created as part of the GTM Systems, Traffic Integrity, and Digital Intelligence expansion wave for the Kinetic Gain portfolio.

## Intent

The goal was to show that form-abuse prevention belongs in the same conversation as pipeline quality and attribution trust. Rather than treating spam capture like isolated hygiene work, the repo turns it into a revenue-facing control plane:

- suspicious submissions get scored before they enter routing and nurture systems
- validation rules are explained in commercial language
- Growth and RevOps can see the cost of dirty lead capture

## Positioning

`honeypot-form-validator` supports the `Traffic Integrity` pillar:

- role level: `Architect`
- signal metric: `92% Threat Mitigation`
- focus: bot protection, fraud prevention, clean traffic pipelines

It also reinforces the broader public story:

- Director of Web & GTM Systems
- Pipeline Scaling & Threat Mitigation
